About this tool

This tool helps researchers detect and quantify selection bias in cross-national studies —it may be used to check whether data availability is systematically related to countries' development levels. Thus, it addresses a widespread but often overlooked methodological problem: assuming missing data is random when it may be actually related to national capacity to collect and report statistics.
